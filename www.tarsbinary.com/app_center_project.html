TARS App Center


This will be the portfolio of TARS Software for apps. All the apps developed in TARS Software and TARS BINARY will be showcased and kept here. Consumers will be led to playstore or AppStore from here to download or install the app. Also, potential consumers can order their desired app of TARS Software from here, so that they can get their apps designed from TARS Software.

Categories of app center


Teen Engineers: Apps designed by youngsters from TARS Engineering School will be showcased here.
Innovative Apps: Apps developed by TARS Software employees.
Service Apps: Apps that TARS Software has developed to provide service to other companies.
Taking Orders for App: Consumers can have their desired app made from here, ordering directly to TARS Software.
