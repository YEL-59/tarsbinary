Glade 4 – Military Service


Glade 4 is an RC military rover that can be accessed from a distance to spy on an enemy or to study a warzone thoroughly. This rover can be controlled from a mobile control station (set in the warzone) and can study an area in detail. This can be used to gain clear conception about the enemy forces, being undetected and unharmed.‘Glade 4’ can collect data and store (or relay) it for digital forensics. It will take digital photographs and keep it authentically stored for the digital forensics team to analyze them.

Problem we are solving


In a particular area where human access is not possible, a rover-bot is recommended. That area can vary from military to rescue forces (fire brigade, bomb squad etc). For remotely controlled rescue or surveillance operations, a device is much needed that can provide all the necessary details of the particular area, and in case of military use, being undetected.

PROJECT Features

Threat Recognition: This rover can detect threats, say, by their appearance and uniforms. It can differentiate ally forces from enemy forces. This feature ensures that from the data received by the rover, enemy location can be determined correctly and not confused with the location of an ally. Further aspects of threat detection will include detailed features of recognizing military vehicles, tanks, landmines and guns/cannons that the enemy forces are equipped with. This will give a clear image of the enemy’s forces.
Distance Meter: After detecting the threats, the rover can calculate/determine their distances compared to itself through its camera, and arrange the data with respect to the control station. Say, the rover moves 1000 meter from the control station and locates an enemy 100 meter ahead of it. Then it will inform the control station that an enemy has been located 1100 meters (in specific direction) from the station.
Location Meter: This feature will plot the locations of every enemy on the GPS map so that strategies can be made upon that data, knowing every entry and exit path through the enemy. This feature is related to the Distance Meter feature, that not only detects threats but also plot them in a GPS map.
Area Detail Coverage: The advanced step of Location Meter is the Area Detail Coverage, that gives an overall clear image of the warzone. It includes the enemy men, their vehicles, artillery, communication devices (with their electro-magnetic signature), drones/rovers or such RC devices with its camera, metal detector, electro-magnetic sensor, thermal detector and AI. It also studies the terrain in detail and takes reading of the obstacles. This feature ensures that the control station gets the best detail of the warzone.
Droid System Jammer: This rover not only works for receiving data, but also it can make some little offences. An important feature is this Droid System Jammer, which can jam electric devices such as electric weapons, communication modules, RC equipment in its path.
Simultaneous Receiving and Sending: This feature fulfills the rover. In an active warzone, it is important to receive simultaneous data from an RC device; to command it anytime and get live data from it about the warzone. This rover is enabled with continuous receiving and sending capability, that ensures that it never gets disconnected or detached from the control station.
Retractable Camera Mechanism For stealth capability, the camera (or input devices/ surveillance devices) will have a retractable mechanism that can keep the camera hidden until needed to be unfolded.
