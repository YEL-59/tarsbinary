Our Story

Our story

we started our journey in the USA, in the year 2014 with the name "Shell Binary". Since then we have been working on cybersecurity, on-demand software development and robotics on a global scale. We moved back to Bangladesh in 2018 and started our operations with the name "TARS Binary". We want to normalize working IT jobs outside of Dhaka and so we set up our headquarters in High Tech Park, Rajshahi.

Goals

We want to provide cybersecurity, software, AI and Robotics solutions that have a lasting positive impact on our society and surroundings. We encourage innovation among our employees. We brainstorm and try to solve those problems that surround us on local, national, even international levels through the power of technology. We want to be a part of the revolution that eradicates poverty in Bangladesh, makes the youth technology-capable and makes the country self sufficient in Technology.
