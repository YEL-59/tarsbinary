Network Project 55RTN


BECAUSE YOUR SAFETY IS YOUR RIGHT, NOT YOUR PRIVILEGE

The number of fatalities due to crime has been rapidly increasing particularly in recent years. There is an urgent need and scope for improving the public safety situation by implementing an effective and integrated safety policy and actions where we can contribute to mitigate these problems together. At TARS Binary, our goal is to empower Bangladesh by delivering what matters- the safety that our citizens deserve. What drives us to deliver the best value for our clients is the passion of liberating them from having to constantly live in the fear of falling victims.

PHYSICAL LAYOUT


Our targeted areas will be enclosed by a virtual geofence allowing us to trigger a response when a mobile device enters or leaves a particular area. We will set up a number of network towers to bring that area under our network system and a central server system where the necessary data collected from the network can be sent to. Security authorities can use this information to match them with pre-stored data sets to determine the criminal. A number of close circuit television (CCTV) cameras will be installed in the important part of the campus for 24/7 monitoring.

PROJECT GOALS

This project is targeted to minimize the crime rate as much as possible to make Bangladesh a better place to live and provide the citizens a surety of a safe environment so that security concerns cannot hold them back from flourishing their dreams.
Quicker Remedy: This project enables the authority to respond faster as the victim can trigger a live alarm and the authority in charge of security is informed immediately. It also ensures a higher probability of rescuing the victim.
Access to Authentic Evidence When turned on, the system takes live pictures and records live voices, which ensures the authenticity of the incidents. These authentic data results in a more accurate investigation. A widespread system like this makes a potential criminal think twice before actually committing a crime.
Reducing Crime Rate: A widespread system like this makes a potential criminal think twice before actually committing a crime. Crime rate drops helps to improve the total quality of life significally.
Safer Campus: This project will ensure a healthy and safe environment for the students that they truly deserve.
Safeguard to citizens: This project makes it easier than ever to save people’s lives in medical emergencies, fire accidents, natural disasters, road accidents, and many more complex situations.
Supporting Law Enforcement Agencies: This project will help the law enforcement agencies of the country a great deal as they can take fast and drastic measures to stop a crime or catch outlaws. Effective activities will revive people’s faith in the law. It will help to make the bond between police and the general public stronger.
Supporting Proctorial Body: Successful installation of this system can come in handy to the proctorial body as they can enhance security on the campus, thus providing a safe and sound educational environment for the students.
Easy Access to Policing Service: Through this service, people can get help directly from law enforcement agencies without going through hassles of police stations.
Increasing Awareness: Proper application of the security system will make people more aware of the crimes happening around them. When people will notice the positive outcome of this service, they will be more eager to solve crimes around them by reporting to the concerned authority.
