The problem we are solving


Why rely on your smartphone always for your work? For office work, reading, taking notes, movie watching or say, presentation, some other device may serve better. A device optimized for those range of works, designed classy and portable is needed in the market which the tech lovers are yearning for. This device is needed in the market for another reason, to replace smartphones. In case of parents who want their children to learn with a smart device but don’t like to provide them with smartphones or phones yet, need this replacement. Also, the chance of timeloss when on a smartphone can be mitigated a bit when office work isn't taken to smartphones when portability is much needed.
