ABOUT TARS Cyeber Security

The government has adopted a cyber-security declaration 2017 asking organizations to develop actionable cyber security road maps to be approved and monitored by the top management. It is evident that necessary measures to ensure cyber security in the country has just been started to be taken, and software firms will pave the way firsthand. TARS Cyber Security wants to participate in the process and lead the way also. Throughout the present projects from TARS Software and TARS Cyber Security, TARS BINARY is on the process of designing a system to ensure people’s safety through technology. Projects like ‘Network 55 RTN’ are being prepared to ensure surveillance over areas to make places safer for people. Cyber-security also makes investigation processes easier, and certain.

TARS Cyber Security is the wing of TARS BINARY that works on this uncommon field in which few progresses has been made in Bangladesh. For that, TARS Cyber Security is cooperating with the other wings of TARS BINARY.

Motivation behind TARS Cyber Security

Bangladesh has ranked 73 out of 100 nations in the global cyber security index measured by UK-based National Cyber Security Index (NCSI). The NCSI put the countries in the ranking after measuring their preparedness to prevent fundamental cyber threats and readiness to manage cyber incidents, crimes and large-scale cyber crises. This situation indicates the huge demand of work on cyber security in Bangladesh. TARS Cyber Security sees this as an opportunity for new IT expert and programmers to dive in. There are evidently a lot of youngsters who are currently working in this sector passionately, as their hobby. These students from colleges and schools do basic facebook account and stuff like this for fun. Given a proper guidance and made connected to the real field, they can do marvel.

The goal of TARS Cyber Security is to connect with such enthusiasts and lead them to join in the sector. Our prime motivation is the vision of a time, when someone will think twice about their actions on the cyber space, whether it will harm someone or not.
